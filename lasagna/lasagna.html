Lasagna

Description

A Hearty family meal made with layers of white sauce mince and pasta sheets enjoyed by many this is a simple dish with little difficulty in making it

Ingredients

    1lb of sweet italian sausage
    3/4 lb of lean ground beef
    1/2 cup of minced onions
    2 cloves of garlic, crushed
    1(28oz) can crushed tomato
    2(6.5oz) cans of tomato sauce
    2(6oz) caans of tomato paste
    1/2 cup of water
    2 tables spoons white sugar
    4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsely
    1&1/2 teaspoons of dried basil leaves
    1&1/2 teaspoons of salt
    1 teaspoon italian seasoning
    1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
    1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
    1 egg
    3/4 lb mozzzarella, sliced
    3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  • Step 1

    Gather all your ingredients

  • Step 2

    Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a dutch oven over medium heat until well browned

  • Step 3

    Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomatoe paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally

  • Step 4>

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. cook lasagna noodles, and rinse with cold water.

  • Step 5>

    In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons of parselt, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt

  • Step 6

    Preheat oven to 190oC (275F)

  • Step 7

    To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over the meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over mozzzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese.

  • Step 8

    Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzzarella and parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.

  • Step 9

    Bakes in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes

  • Step 10

    Rest Lasagna for 15 minutes before serving
